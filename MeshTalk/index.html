MeshTalk - Elegant mesh is art

by willowfly | resident of Never-Never-Land

Talking about the mesh, people will all agree that this is the most effortful, important pre-work to a good finite element simulation. But I think an elegant mesh is not just a skillful task, but also some kind of art. In MeshTalk, i'd like to discuss my considerations on generating and manipulating finite element grids.
